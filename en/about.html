About Casa Tile

For over 15 years we've brought Moldova the finest ceramic tiles from Europe.

Casa Tile specializes in ceramic tiles, porcelain stoneware and mosaics for residential and commercial spaces. We work directly with manufacturers in Italy, Spain and Poland to bring our customers high-quality products at fair prices.

Our team of consultants supports you at every step — from choosing the right model for your project, to calculating quantities and organizing delivery. We have a modern showroom where you can see the products in person.

Whether you're renovating a bathroom, building a new home or working on a commercial project, we're here to help.
